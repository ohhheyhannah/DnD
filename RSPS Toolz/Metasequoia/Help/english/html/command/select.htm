Select

It select vertices, lines and faces on the view. Several method for selection can be switched.

Normal

It selects a vertex, line or face normally.

You can select or deselect continually with dragging of left button. (Cannot select a line.)

Joined face

All joined faces with clicked vertex or face are selected or unselected. (Cannot select a line.)

Belt

Click a line, and it selects or unselects belted vertices and faces. It selects lines when the [Fc] button in the edit option is not pushed.

Edge loop

It continuously selects lines joined to the clicked or dragged line. When the line has been clicked, lines from the clicked to the farthest are selected together. When the line has been dragged, it selects while gradually spread.

Growing

It selects vertices/faces while gradually spread by dragging.

Route

Please click two or more vertices, lines or faces. And vertices, lines or faces on the shortest path through the clicked points are selected. Please click a right button or double click when you want to finish the selection.

CurObj

The object that contains the clicked vertex, line, or face is made current.

Brush

The vertices traced by dragging are selected.
