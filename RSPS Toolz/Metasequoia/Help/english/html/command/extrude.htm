Extrd (Extrude)

It extrudes selected faces.

It pushes out with dragging to the right, and dents with dragging to the left with pressing the left button.

This command is similar to menu - Selected - 'Extrude faces' but this can inputs the amount of extrude visually.

Normal

It extrudes faces normally. It makes new joined faces when two selected faces are joined.

Bevel

It makes tapered faces. It makes new separated faces even if two selected faces are joined.
