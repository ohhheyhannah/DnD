Wire

Wire command in each option brings different operation. It can only be done by dragging or single click in all cases.

in descending order

[Create a triangle]
[Divide a quadrangle]
[Invert a vertex]
[Swap a line two triangles share]
[Merge two triangles into a quadrangle]

Create a triangle

Drag a mouse like joining each vertex at the other side of the vertex which two lines share and you can create a new triangle. Or, you can also make it by dragging an existing line .

Divide a quadrangle

Drag a mouse between facing two vertices of a quadrangle and you can divide it into two triangles.

Insert a vertex

Click an existing line and you can create some new triangles.

Swap a line two triangles share

Click a line two triangles share and you can swap lines.

Merge two triangles into a quadrangle

Click a line two triangles share and you can erase the line and create a new quadrangle.
