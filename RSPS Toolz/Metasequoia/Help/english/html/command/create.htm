Create

It puts vertices, and creates a face.

If you click a left button with Ctrl-key, the new face shares a vertex contained by the existing face. If you don't push Ctrl-key, it's influenced by 'Distance to select' in the configuration.

If you click with Shift-key for 2nd or later vertex, a vertex moved only horizontally or vertically from the previous one will be put.

Front

It creates a front side of face.

Back

It creates a back side of face.

Both

It creates a both side of face.

Unlike a double-sided face in the material property, two faces are actually created.

Line

It creates a line when two points are put.

Tri (Triangle)

It creates a triangle when three points are put.

Face

It creates a quadrangle when four points are put. And it creates a line or a triangle when two or three points are put and click the right button.

Undo

It erases the point put last.
