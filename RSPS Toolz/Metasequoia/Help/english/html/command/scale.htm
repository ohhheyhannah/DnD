Scale

It moves selected vertices, lines and faces to scale.

Scale

Handle
It shows three arrows along with X/Y/Z axes, and you can scale vertices, lines and faces to X/Y/Z axis direction with dragging of left button.
Proportional
It scales in the same ratio for everyone of X, Y and Z axes.
Screen
It scales in the ratio in the length and breadth of externals on the screen. It is necessary to note that the actual shape are sheared on the perspective view.
X/Y/Z
It scales to either of X, Y or Z axis direction.
Number
Input a number of scaling rate along with X/Y/Z axes.
You can specify a scaling rate and a method to scale in the number dialog.
Scaling
It specifies scaling factors on X, Y and Z axes by numbers.
Rate
It specifies a scaling factor by a rate.
Size
It specifies a scaling factor by a size for a selected part.
Initialize
It restores scaling factors to 1.
Center
It specifies a base position for scaling on X, Y and Z axes.
Zero
It restores a base position to 0.
Local
It restores a base position to a center of a local coordinate of a current object.
Selected
It restores a base position to a center of selected vertices, lines and faces.
