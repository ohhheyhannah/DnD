Preview

It can show faces and texture using any of software, Direct3D, or OpenGL. This can make it easier to know the shape visually.

In Direct3D or OpenGL mode, various problems might occur because of trouble or the compatibility of the display driver. In that case, please see FAQ. I recommend you to use the latest driver offered by the chip board maker.

The Direct3D mode is better on the most PCs because of a speed and a quality.

Software

  • This is an original render of Metasequoia. This is available for any PC. In old PCs which do not work with Direct3D or OpenGL in hardware level, it displays fastest.
  • A bump mapping isn't displayed.

Direct3D

  • The video card needs to support hardware 3D rendering. Direct3D is available on the almost all PCs sold in the year 2012. However, with notebook computers sold in several years ago, or in the virtual environment without a support for Direct3D, you may not be able to use Direct3D.
  • Textures cannot be shown on three orthogonal views.

OpenGL

  • Some troubles may come with the display driver contained in the graphics card (or built-in chips) except Intel/ATI/nVIDIA. Please use the reliable video card authorized by major 3DCG software as much as possible. And please update the driver to the latest version offered by the maker.
  • A bump mapping isn't displayed.
